About Shubhayatra

Understanding Nepal's Tourism

Who We Are

We're rethinking travel experiences in Nepal. Using the power of design thinking, we explore Nepali tourism through real conversations, empathy-driven research, and hands-on design. From sketching out ideas, building user-friendly prototypes and shaping sustainable business models, we aim to gently innovate local and international travel experiences in Nepal.

What We Do

We conduct detailed surveys and interviews with tourists visiting Nepal to understand their experiences, challenges, and suggestions. Our goal is to identify recurring problems and develop practical solutions.

Why It Matters

Tourism is vital for Nepal's economy. By solving tourist problems, we help improve Nepal's reputation as a world-class destination while supporting local communities.
